Rules
Rules in this prototype:
• Player 1 and Player 2 are real players online.
• Move 1 tile per click until your rolled moves run out.
• No diagonal movement.
• You cannot move onto the other player’s tile or wall tiles.
• A player is trapped if they have no legal adjacent tile to move to on their turn.
• If you end your turn orthogonally adjacent to the opponent, you win the round.
• First to 2 round wins takes the match.
• You can also move using Arrow Keys or WASD.
